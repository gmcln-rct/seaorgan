seaOrganOf


seaOrganOf uses Tides & Currents data from NOAA to generate unique audio soundscapes for coastal stations around the United States.

The organ translates 24 hours of sea level data into a unique set of musical notes and plays them in sequence.

Select a U.S. coastal station, then click Play Organ to begin listening.

seaOrganOf

seaOrganOf is inspired by theSea Organ of Zadarin Croatia, which uses the movement of the seas to create ethereal, organ-like sounds.

This project aims to create an immersive audio ecperience by using NOAA's tides & currents data for locations around the coastal U.S. to generate audio soundscapes specific to location. Select your location, click play and enjoy!

Created by: Glenn McClanan

Technologies: Tone.js

Inspirations: discreetmusic.js by Tero Parviainen
Source Data: NOAA

Best experienced with headphones.
(Audio optimized for desktop/laptop, disabled on iOS.)
